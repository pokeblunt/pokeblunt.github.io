Rules

We generally follow Smogon's Doubles OU rules and play several tournaments where each player participates each season. Playoffs are held at the end of each season.

Battle rules

Use Smogon's Gen 6 (X and Y) Double OU rules with the following additions and exceptions:

  • Pokemon restrictions. Pokemon with greater than 600 base stats cannot be drafted:
    • Any pokemon with > 600 base stats
  • Legendary restrictions. Pokemon considered to be legendary cannot enter battle:
    • Any legendary pokemon
  • Move restrictions. Pokemon cannot use these moves:
    • Dark Void
  • Move allowed. All moves that are not restricted are allowedd including these banned by Smogon:
    • Fissure
    • Guillotine
    • Horn Drill
    • Sheer Cold
    • Swagger
  • Ability allowed: Pokemon can have these abilities:
    • Moody
  • Items allowed. All items are allowed including these banned by Smogon:
    • Gengarite
    • Kangaskhanite
  • Roster clause: Players can only use Pokemon from their roster.

Tournament rules

Use the following rules for each tournament:

  1. Each player chooses a team of 6 pokemon to use the entire tournament.
  2. Each player plays all other players twice.
  3. The player who won the most battles wins the tournament. If two or more players are tied with the most wins, all other players are eliminated and the remaining players repeat steps 2 and 3.

Season rules

Players play 14 tournaments. The top two players advance to the championship. Players are ranked by tournament wins and then number of matches won if tied. If still tied, play single matches to determine an order. The championship is a best 3 out of 5. The champion wins the season and picks their MVP (Most Valuable Pokemon) for the season.
