Rules

Draft rules

All pokemon are chosen at random by opening 2 packs of Scarlet and Violet 151 pokemon cards. Duplicate pokemon are allowed within and across teams.

Trade rules

You can trade pokemon with other players. If you have 2 of the same pokemon on your team you can choose to trade it in for the next stage in evolution.

Battle rules

Use Smogon's Gen 9 Double OU rules with the following additions and exceptions.

  1. 151 clause. Players can only use the Kanto version of the first 151 Pokemon.
  2. Roster clause. Players can only use Pokemon from their roster.
  3. Tera clause. Terastalization is not allowed.
  4. Previous generations clause. Pokemon from previous generations 1 - 8 are allowed. Also any moves learned by a pokemon in previous generations are allowed even if they cannot be learned in gen 9.
    • Pokemon from previous generations
    • Pokemon moves from previous generations
  5. Abilities clause. The following abilities are allowed.
    • Moody
    • Shadow Tag
  6. Evasion clause: Players can use the moves
    • Double Team
    • Minimize
  7. OHKO clause: Players can use the moves
    • Fissure
    • Guillotine
    • Horn Drill
    • Sheer Cold
  8. Gravity Sleep Clause: Players can use any move with Gravity
    • Gravity

Tournament rules

Players can play any number of matches against each other. ELO will automatically be calculated.

Season rules

All players advance to the playoffs. Players are ranked by ELO. If still tied, play single matches to determine an order.

Playoff rules

Players can use different pokemon for each match even within one playoff round. Seed a 6 person single elimination bracket (top 2 players get byes). The first round is best 2 out of 3. The semi-finals are best 3 out of 5. The finals and 3rd vs 4th place match are best 4 our of 7. The champion of the finals wins the season and picks their MVP (Most Valuable Pokemon) for the season.
